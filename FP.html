About me

Welcome to the captivating world of photography through the lens of a seasoned artist with over 15 years of experience. Based in the vibrant city of New York, I specialize in a diverse range of photography genres, from private events to wildlife, and fashion.

My journey behind the camera has led me to capture moments of raw emotion, fleeting beauty, and untamed nature, all against the iconic backdrop of the Big Apple. Whether it's freezing the energy of a bustling New York Fashion Week runway, preserving the grace of Central Park's dance skaters, or immortalizing the delicate elegance of our avian friends in flight, I strive to evoke a sense of wonder and connection through my work.

Having collaborated with esteemed clients such as the Central Park Conservancy, DAZN, and PRIME, I bring a wealth of experience and creativity to every project. My passion for photography knows no bounds, fueling my relentless pursuit of the perfect shot and my commitment to delivering images that resonate deeply with viewers.

Join me on this visual journey as we explore the beauty, diversity, and excitement of life through the eyes of a photographer dedicated to capturing moments that transcend time.
